Bio

I'm PV — The Clumsy Boy. Founder. Tinkerer. Restless by nature, accidental by design.

I've built a career out of doing things wrong until they go right. I was clumsy once — really committed to it — and then somehow managed to pull it off again. Twice. The second time wasn't even planned, which is honestly the most on-brand thing about me.

I don't have a five-year plan. I have a bias for action, a high tolerance for stumbling, and a weird belief that the best way to figure something out is to just start. If you're into building things, breaking things, or watching someone do both at the same time — we'll get along just fine.
